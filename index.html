The Origin

Founded on Partnership

Big2Detailing is more than just a car detailing business—it is the result of ambition, friendship, hard work, and the desire to create something meaningful from the ground up. Founded by two friends with a shared vision, we operate as a mobile service serving the Triangle Area of North Carolina.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you need access to my water or power?

While we are fully mobile, access to an exterior water spigot and a standard electrical outlet allows us to work more efficiently and keep costs lower for you!

How long does a full detail take?

Depending on the condition of the vehicle, a Signature Detail typically takes 3-5 hours for a thorough job.

What if it rains on the day of my appointment?

If you have a garage we can work in, we can proceed! If not, we will contact you to reschedule at your earliest convenience.
